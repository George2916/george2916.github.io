THE LIVES OF AIDAN AND HARRISON: AN UNCLE'S PERSPECTIVE

Aidan Charles Greenslade is born on April 20, 2000, the son of Steve and Lori Greenslade. His middle name is the name of his mother's brother. He has two older sisters, Haley and Abigail. Famous people born on April 20th include fascist Adolf Hitler and Supreme Court Justice John Paul Stevens.

In May 2000, Aidan and his family visit Uncle George and Aunt Sunghee in New York.(1)(2) Aidan is (a) barely sentient and (b) unaware of their existence.

(2) May 2000

In July 2000, Uncle George and Aunt Sunghee attend an event involving water sorcery, as a result of which Aidan is deemed baptized.(3) This is the first of many rituals that Aidan will undergo as a member of what some aver, with evidence most unpersuasive, is the one true church

In July 2001, Uncle George and Aunt Sunghee meet Aidan and his family in Buffalo at his paternal grandparents' home. The occasion is the celebration of Aidan's great-grandmother's 90th birthday.(4) A photograph taken on the occasion shows four generations of Greenslades.

In July 2002, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George meet Aidan and his family in Buffalo at his paternal grandparents' home. A photo taken on the occassion shows a visibly upset Aidan. His cousin Colin and his sisters, Haley and Abigail, appear more sanguine.(6)
